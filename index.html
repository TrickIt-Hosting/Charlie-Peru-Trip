My Story

I'm Charlie, and I've been accepted for the World Challenge expedition to Peru. This is an incredible opportunity to explore new cultures, challenge myself, and make a positive impact through community projects.

"I'm combining my passion for 3D printing with my fundraising goal to create something meaningful."

Rather than just asking for donations, I've decided to offer custom 3D printing services. Every product I create helps fund my journey while providing you with unique, personalized items.

The £5,000 I'm raising will cover expedition costs, equipment, insurance, and allow me to contribute to community projects in Peru. Your support makes this adventure possible.
